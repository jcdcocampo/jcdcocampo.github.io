About

I'm a licensed Electronics Engineer and Electronics Technician, and a fresh graduate from Mapua University, where I specialized in Advanced Internet Protocol Networking. During my studies, I completed an internship at Converge ICT Solutions as an IT Helpdesk Intern, where I gained experience resolving and escalating Level 1 IT concerns while strengthening my understanding of enterprise network support operations. I also contributed to IEEE-recognized research through my thesis on neural network applications for real-time sensor data prediction.

Outside of my core engineering background, I have a strong interest in cloud computing and artificial intelligence, which I am actively pursuing alongside my networking foundation.
